Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Lack of healthcare, the shares of smoking obese population all correlate with povery level.

Median age negatively correlates with share of population without health coverage, i.e., younger people seem to lackhealthcare the most. The median age positively correlates with the % smokers. There is no significant correlation between median age and obesity rate.

Median household income negatively correlates with the lack of healthcare, % of smokers and obesity rates. It is safe to conclude that people with higher median household income are healthier overall due to better access to healthcare.
